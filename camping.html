Family Camping

Established campgrounds within 200 miles of Reno that can host multiple rigs in the same area

Trip in planning: August 6–9, 2026 Three rigs need sites in the same campground — close together, but they don't need to touch:
  • The Green family — 35ft motorhome
  • Mom & Dad — 32ft travel trailer
  • Chris — 12ft cargo trailer

River, creek, or lake all work. Full hookups preferred but not required — a great dry-camping spot still works. Private RV resorts (own phone/online booking) are the realistic path a month out; USFS/state campgrounds booked through Recreation.gov or ReserveCalifornia run on a 6-month advance-booking window and August at Lake Tahoe sells out within minutes of release.

Live-checked 2026-07-07 on Recreation.gov: Zephyr Cove Resort has zero RV sites with all of Aug 6–7–8 open at once (only scattered single-night gaps), and Nevada Beach Campground is fully booked the entire month. Treat federal/state campgrounds as a cancellation-watch, not the primary plan — Zephyr Cove on Recreation.gov  |  Nevada Beach on Recreation.gov. The private resorts below aren't subject to that rush and are the better bet.
